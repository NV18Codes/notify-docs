# Carousel Templates

Carousel templates let you send swipeable WhatsApp messages with multiple cards in a single template.  
This format works well when you want to show several products, offers, or services in one message while keeping the experience visual and easy to interact with.

---

## Navigate and Create

The initial setup is the same as standard templates.

1. Go to **Engagement → WhatsApp Templates**
2. Click **Create Template**
3. Enter **Template Name**, **Category**, and **Language**
4. Choose **Carousel** format in supported template type options
5. Add cards, media, text, and buttons
6. Review and submit for approval

## 01. Template Type and Eligibility

Carousel is supported only under the **Marketing** category.  
You cannot create carousel templates in **Utility** or **Authentication**.

<WarningBox>
  If the category is not Marketing, carousel options may not be available or may fail approval.
</WarningBox>

---

## 02. Media Support and Card Limits

Carousel templates support up to **10 cards** in one template.  
Each card can include media and text content.

Rules to follow:

- Each card can use **image** or **video**
- Do not mix image and video types within the same carousel template
- Media files must follow WhatsApp size and format requirements

---

## 03. Buttons Configuration

Each card can contain up to **2 buttons**.

Supported button types:

- Call-to-Action (Visit Website / Call)
- Quick Reply

By default, button structure is applied across all cards.  
You can customize button text at card level where needed.

> **Note:** After approval, button structure (count/type) cannot be changed.

---

## 04. Card-Level Customization

You can customize each card individually to make the carousel more relevant.

Each card can have:

- Different media
- Different header text
- Different body content

This makes carousel templates ideal for showcasing multiple products or service plans in one outbound message.

---

## 05. Content Guidelines

Carousel messages are designed for quick visual browsing.  
For better performance and readability:

- Keep card text short and clear
- Focus on one offer or message per card
- Use strong action text in buttons
- Avoid long paragraphs inside card body content

---

## 06. Use Cases

Carousel templates are commonly used for:

- Product showcases
- Offers and discounts
- Real estate listings
- Event promotions
- Service package highlights

---

## 07. Approval and Compliance

Like all WhatsApp templates, carousel templates must be approved before they can be used.

To improve approval success:

- Avoid misleading claims
- Avoid restricted or non-compliant content
- Keep brand representation clear and consistent

> **Note:** You cannot dynamically add more cards after a template is approved.

---

## 08. User Experience Behavior

Customers swipe horizontally to view cards one by one.

Because the first card gets the highest attention:

- Put your best product or strongest offer in card 1
- Keep visual style consistent across all cards
- Keep CTA intent clear and easy to understand

---

## 09. Best Practices

- Use high-quality images or videos
- Maintain consistent branding (colors, tone, product style)
- Highlight key information in the first 2 cards
- Use action-driven button labels like **Buy Now**, **Explore**, or **Book Today**

<TipBox>
  A well-structured carousel often performs better than a single long promotional message because customers can scan and act faster.
</TipBox>

---

## 10. Example Scenario

An electronics retailer wants to promote weekend offers in one message.  
The team creates a **Marketing** carousel template with 5 cards:

1. Card 1: Best-selling smartphone with headline `Weekend Mega Deal`
2. Card 2: Smartwatch offer with short benefit-focused text
3. Card 3: Wireless earbuds bundle
4. Card 4: Laptop discount card
5. Card 5: Accessory combo offer

Each card uses image media, keeps body text under two short lines, and includes two CTA buttons:

- `Explore`
- `Buy Now`

They keep design style consistent across all cards and place the strongest offer in card 1.  
After approval, the template is used in a campaign for recent shoppers, resulting in higher click-through compared to a standard single-message format.

---

## Summary

Carousel templates are a high-impact marketing format for WhatsApp when you need to present multiple options in one message.  
If you keep media consistent, copy concise, and CTA-focused, carousel campaigns can improve both engagement and conversion quality.
