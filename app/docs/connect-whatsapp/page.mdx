# Connect WhatsApp Business API

Connect your WhatsApp account so Notifyy can register webhooks, sync templates, and send on your behalf.

![Connect — Meta portfolio and WABA](/docs/onboarding/step-3.png)

![Connect — workspace connected](/docs/onboarding/step-4.png)

## When to use

Use this when you are setting Notifyy up for the first time or reconnecting after Meta changed portfolio access.

## Steps

<StepBlock>

1. Sign in to Notifyy and open **Settings → WhatsApp** (wording may vary slightly by release).
2. Start **Connect** and sign in to the **Meta Business Portfolio** that owns the WhatsApp asset.
3. Select or create a **WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)** and assign a **phone number** you control.
4. Wait until the workspace shows **Connected**. This usually takes a few minutes; queued jobs on Meta side can stretch longer.
5. Sync **message templates** and confirm they appear as **approved** or **pending** — not an empty list.

</StepBlock>

## Notes

<WarningBox>
  Your number must not already be tied to another WhatsApp Business API integration you still rely on. Moving numbers is possible but slow; pick the line you intend to keep.
</WarningBox>

<TipBox>
  Use a dedicated operations or support number instead of a founder personal line — fewer surprises when volume spikes.
</TipBox>

If the OAuth screen fails or shows the wrong portfolio, log out of Meta in another tab and retry. If verification fails, wait about **5 minutes** before trying again — rapid retries often get throttled.

## Example

A business connects **+91 98xx xxxxxx**, syncs template **`order_shipped_en`**, and only then grants marketing access to audiences.
