# WhatsApp onboarding & setup

Notifyy helps you complete **WhatsApp API integration** and move from setup to your first **WhatsApp campaign**.

👉 [Go to Notifyy Dashboard](https://www.notifyy.io/)

---

## WhatsApp API integration (overview)

- Connect your WhatsApp Business account  
- Verify your business  
- Create and approve templates  
- Import contacts  
- Run your first **WhatsApp campaign setup**  

---

## Step 1: Connect WhatsApp

Go to **Settings → WhatsApp → Connect Account**.

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-1.png" alt="Connect WhatsApp account in Notifyy dashboard" />

<p className="text-xs text-gray-500 mt-1 dark:text-zinc-400">Step preview from dashboard</p>

### Steps

1. Click **Connect Account**  
2. Enter business details  
3. Add phone number  
4. Complete OTP verification  

<div className="bg-yellow-50 border border-yellow-200 p-3 rounded-md text-sm dark:bg-yellow-950/30 dark:border-yellow-800">
⚠️ Your number must not be linked to another WhatsApp API.
</div>

---

## Step 2: Verify Business

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-5.png" alt="Verify business in Notifyy" />

<p className="text-xs text-gray-500 mt-1 dark:text-zinc-400">Step preview from dashboard</p>

### Steps

1. Enter business details  
2. Submit documents  
3. Wait for approval  

⏱️ Usually a few minutes to a few hours.

---

## Step 3: Create Template

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-9.png" alt="Create WhatsApp template" />

<p className="text-xs text-gray-500 mt-1 dark:text-zinc-400">Step preview from dashboard</p>

### Steps

1. Go to **Templates**  
2. Click **Create Template**  
3. Add message  
4. Submit  

<div className="bg-yellow-50 border border-yellow-200 p-3 rounded-md text-sm dark:bg-yellow-950/30 dark:border-yellow-800">
⚠️ Template must be approved before sending campaigns
</div>

---

## Step 4: Import Contacts

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-13.png" alt="Import contacts CSV" />

<p className="text-xs text-gray-500 mt-1 dark:text-zinc-400">Step preview from dashboard</p>

### Steps

1. Go to **Contacts → Import**  
2. Upload CSV  
3. Map fields  
4. Save  

<div className="bg-green-50 border border-green-200 p-3 rounded-md text-sm dark:bg-green-950/25 dark:border-green-800">
💡 Tip: Include country code in phone numbers.
</div>

---

## Step 5: Send First Campaign

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-18.png" alt="Send first WhatsApp campaign" />

<p className="text-xs text-gray-500 mt-1 dark:text-zinc-400">Step preview from dashboard</p>

### Steps

1. Go to **Campaigns**  
2. Select template  
3. Choose audience  
4. Click **Send**  

<div className="bg-green-50 border border-green-200 p-3 rounded-md text-sm dark:bg-green-950/25 dark:border-green-800">
💡 Tip: Start with a small audience first.
</div>

---

## Common Issues

- **Template not approved** → Cannot send  
- **Wrong phone format** → Messages fail  
- **API not connected** → Campaign disabled
