# Customer Onboarding

Set up Notifyy to connect your WhatsApp Business account, configure messaging capabilities, and start engaging with your customers.

👉 [Access Notifyy Platform](https://www.notifyy.io/)

_Last updated: Recently · Estimated time: 5–7 minutes_

---

## Overview

The onboarding process prepares your account for WhatsApp communication and ensures compliance with WhatsApp Business requirements.

By the end of this setup, you will be able to:

- Send WhatsApp campaigns  
- Manage customer contacts  
- Use approved message templates  
- Track communication through the dashboard  

---

## Step 1: Connect WhatsApp Business Account

Navigate to **Settings → WhatsApp → Connect Account**

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-1.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Connect WhatsApp — step 1" />
<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-2.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Connect WhatsApp — step 2" />

### What this step does

This step links your WhatsApp number with Notifyy, enabling message sending and receiving.

### Steps

1. Click **Connect Account**
2. Enter your business details  
3. Add your WhatsApp phone number  
4. Complete OTP verification  

⚠️ **Important**  
The phone number must not be registered with another WhatsApp Business API provider.

💡 **Recommendation**  
Use a dedicated business number instead of a personal number to avoid conflicts.

## Step 2: Verify Your Business

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-5.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Verify business — step 1" />
<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-6.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Verify business — step 2" />

### What this step does

Verification confirms your business identity and enables access to full WhatsApp features.

### Steps

1. Enter your business name and category  
2. Upload required documents (if prompted)  
3. Submit verification request  

⏱️ **Processing time**  
Verification typically takes a few minutes but may take longer depending on submission accuracy.

⚠️ **Common issue**  
If verification fails, ensure your business details exactly match official records.

## Step 3: Create WhatsApp Message Templates

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-9.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Templates — step 1" />
<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-10.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Templates — step 2" />

### What this step does

Templates are required for initiating conversations with customers.

### Steps

1. Navigate to **Templates → Create Template**  
2. Select template type (Marketing, Utility, Authentication)  
3. Add message content and placeholders  
4. Submit for approval  

⚠️ **Important**  
Templates must be approved before they can be used in campaigns.

💡 **Best practice**  
Keep messages clear and concise to improve approval chances.

## Step 4: Import and Manage Contacts

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-13.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Import contacts — step 1" />
<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-14.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Import contacts — step 2" />

### What this step does

This step allows you to upload and organize your customer data.

### Steps

1. Go to **Contacts → Import**  
2. Upload a CSV file containing customer details  
3. Map fields such as Name, Phone Number, and Tags  
4. Save the import  

⚠️ **Important**  
Ensure all phone numbers include the country code (e.g., +91 for India).

💡 **Tip**  
Start with a small dataset to verify mapping before importing large lists.

## Step 5: Create and Send Your First Campaign

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-18.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Campaign — step 1" />
<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-19.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" alt="Campaign — step 2" />

### What this step does

Campaigns allow you to send messages to multiple customers using approved templates.

### Steps

1. Navigate to **Campaigns → Create Campaign**  
2. Select an approved template  
3. Choose your target audience (contacts or segments)  
4. Review and click **Send**  

💡 **Recommendation**  
Start with a small campaign before scaling to a larger audience.

⚠️ **Important**  
If your template is not approved, the campaign cannot be sent.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
|------|------|--------|
| Campaign not sending | Template not approved | Wait for approval |
| Messages failing | Incorrect phone format | Add country code |
| API not connected | Setup incomplete | Reconnect WhatsApp |
