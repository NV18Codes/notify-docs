# Customer Onboarding

Set up Notifyy to start sending WhatsApp messages, managing contacts, and automating workflows.

<hr />

## When to use

Use this guide when setting up Notifyy for the first time.

<hr />

## Overview

The onboarding process includes:

1. Connect your WhatsApp Business account
2. Verify your business
3. Set up templates
4. Import contacts
5. Send your first campaign

<hr />

## Step 1: Connect WhatsApp Business API

Go to **Settings → WhatsApp → Connect Account**

![Connect WhatsApp — start integration](/docs/onboarding/step-1.png)

![Connect WhatsApp — number and verification](/docs/onboarding/step-2.png)

### Steps

1. Click **Connect Account**
2. Enter your business details
3. Add your phone number
4. Complete OTP verification

<WarningBox>
  Your number must not be linked to another WhatsApp Business API.
</WarningBox>

<TipBox>
  Use a dedicated business number instead of a personal number.
</TipBox>

<hr />

## Step 2: Verify Business Account

Verification ensures your account is approved for messaging.

![Verify business — submit details](/docs/onboarding/step-5.png)

![Verify business — review status](/docs/onboarding/step-6.png)

### Steps

1. Submit business documents
2. Enter business name and category
3. Wait for approval

_This usually takes a few minutes to a few hours._

<WarningBox>
  If verification fails, recheck your business details and retry.
</WarningBox>

<hr />

## Step 3: Create WhatsApp Template

Templates are required for sending outbound messages.

![Templates — compose message](/docs/onboarding/step-9.png)

![Templates — submit for approval](/docs/onboarding/step-10.png)

### Steps

1. Go to **Templates → Create Template**
2. Select template type
3. Add message content
4. Submit for approval

<TipBox>
  Keep templates simple to get faster approval.
</TipBox>

<hr />

## Step 4: Import Contacts

Upload your customer data before sending campaigns.

![Import contacts — upload file](/docs/onboarding/step-13.png)

![Import contacts — map fields](/docs/onboarding/step-14.png)

### Steps

1. Go to **Contacts → Import**
2. Upload CSV file
3. Map fields (Name, Phone, etc.)
4. Save

<WarningBox>
  Ensure phone numbers include country code.
</WarningBox>

<hr />

## Step 5: Send Your First Campaign

You are now ready to send your first message.

![Campaign — audience and template](/docs/onboarding/step-18.png)

![Campaign — send and monitor](/docs/onboarding/step-19.png)

### Steps

1. Go to **Campaigns → Create Campaign**
2. Select template
3. Choose audience (segment or contacts)
4. Click **Send**

<TipBox>
  Start with a small audience before scaling.
</TipBox>

<hr />

## Example

Send a promotional message to 100 customers using a pre-approved template.

<hr />

## Common Issues

- Template not approved → Cannot send campaign
- Invalid phone numbers → Messages fail
- API not connected → Campaign option disabled
