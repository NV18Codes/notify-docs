# Customer Onboarding

Setting up Notifyy correctly ensures that your WhatsApp communication works smoothly from the beginning. This process connects your WhatsApp Business account, prepares your messaging setup, and allows you to start engaging with your customers.

👉 [Access Notifyy Platform](https://www.notifyy.io/)

_Last updated: Recently · Estimated time: 6–8 minutes_

---

## Overview

During onboarding, you will complete a series of steps that prepare your account for real usage. Each step is important and builds on the previous one.

By the end of this process, you will be able to send messages, manage contacts, and track campaign performance.

## Step 1: Connect WhatsApp Business Account

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-1.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" />
<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-2.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" />
<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-3.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" />

### What this step means

This step connects your WhatsApp number to Notifyy. Without this connection, you will not be able to send or receive any messages.

### What you need to do

1. Click on **Connect Account**
2. Enter your business details
3. Add your WhatsApp phone number
4. Complete OTP verification

<WarningBox>
  <strong>Warning:</strong> The phone number must not already be connected to another WhatsApp Business API provider.
</WarningBox>

> **Note:** Keep your phone nearby to complete OTP verification.

<TipBox>
  Use a dedicated business number instead of a personal number.
</TipBox>

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-8.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" />

<img src="/docs/onboarding/step-10.png" className="mx-auto rounded-lg border border-gray-200 shadow-sm my-6" />

### What this step means

Templates are required to send outbound messages to customers.

### What you need to do

1. Go to Templates
2. Click Create Template
3. Select type
4. Add your message content
5. Submit the template for approval

<WarningBox>
  <strong>Warning:</strong> Campaigns cannot be sent without approved templates.
</WarningBox>

> **Note:** Approval time depends on WhatsApp review.

<TipBox>
  Keep templates clear and simple for faster approval.
</TipBox>

### What this step means

This step adds your customer data into Notifyy for messaging.

### What you need to do

1. Go to **Contacts → Import**
2. Upload CSV file
3. Map fields
4. Confirm the import

<WarningBox>
  <strong>Warning:</strong> If phone numbers are missing country codes, messages will fail.
</WarningBox>

> **Note:** Ensure CSV format is correct before uploading.

<TipBox>
  Test with a small dataset before importing large lists.
</TipBox>
