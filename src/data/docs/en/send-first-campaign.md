# Send your first campaign

Send a template-based campaign after WABA and templates are ready.

## Before you send

1. Confirm **opt-in** for the audience and template category.
2. Choose an **approved template** in Notifyy.
3. Select or create a **segment** (saved filter on contacts).

## Steps

1. Go to **Campaigns → Create** (or **New campaign**).
2. Select the **template** and **language**.
3. Map template variables to contact fields or static values.
4. Choose **audience** (segment or list).
5. **Schedule** or send **now**.
6. Open **Analytics** (or the campaign detail view) to review delivery and read status.

## After the send

- Inspect failed sends (invalid numbers, template mismatch) in the campaign report.
- Adjust segments or template copy for the next run.
