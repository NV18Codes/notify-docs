# Introduction

Notifyy is a **WhatsApp Business Platform** workspace: campaigns, chatbots, CRM contacts, team inbox, and analytics in one product.

## What this documentation covers

- **Getting started** — connect WhatsApp and send your first campaign.
- **Core features** — campaigns, templates, automation, CRM, inbox, reporting.
- **API & webhooks** — integrate your backend and receive events.

## Requirements

- A **Meta Business Portfolio** and permission to manage the WhatsApp Business Account (WABA).
- **Approved message templates** for categories you use (marketing, utility, authentication).
- **Opt-in** from end users where required for your use case.
