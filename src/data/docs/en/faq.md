# Frequently asked questions

## Compliance & opt-in

**Do I need opt-in before messaging?**  
Yes. WhatsApp requires user consent for the **type** of messages you send (transactional vs promotional). Store proof of opt-in where regulators expect it.

**Can I buy contact lists?**  
No — purchased lists violate policy and hurt deliverability. Import only contacts who expect your messages.

## Deliverability

**Why are my templates rejected?**  
Common reasons: misleading offers, excessive caps, or mismatched languages. Edit in Meta, then re-sync in Notifyy.

**How do I improve CTR?**  
Personalize with variables, shorten CTAs, and pair broadcasts with [QR & interactive messaging](/docs/features/qr-interactive).

## Billing

**Where do I see conversation charges?**  
Navigate to **Billing → Usage** for per-category breakdowns and CSV export.

**Are failed messages charged?**  
WhatsApp generally bills on delivered template opens per policy; see your workspace invoice notes for the exact rule set applied to your WABA.

## Support

**How do I reach Notifyy?**  
Use in-app chat or email **support@notifyy.com** with your workspace ID for fastest routing.
