# WhatsApp campaign guide

Launch high-performing broadcasts that respect policy and your brand voice.

![Customer messaging on mobile — the channel your campaigns reach](/docs/images/whatsapp-mobile-messaging.jpg "WhatsApp-style mobile messaging — illustrative imagery for campaign delivery.")

## Before you send

1. Confirm **opt-in** for the message category.
2. Pick an **approved marketing template** with concise copy.
3. Segment your audience — smaller, relevant cohorts outperform batch-and-blast.

## During the send

- Stagger large audiences to protect **quality rating**.
- Monitor **errors** (invalid numbers, template mismatch) in real time.

## After the send

Compare **read rate**, **CTR**, and **conversions** vs previous sends. Iterate creative and CTA placement — WhatsApp’s **50–70% click** band is achievable when offers are relevant.
