# Lead tracking

Attribute WhatsApp touches to pipeline stages and revenue outcomes.

## Events

Capture **template clicks**, **chatbot completions**, and **agent-tagged outcomes** as structured events.

## Attribution

- **First-touch** and **last-touch** models for campaign sources.
- Export to your BI tool via **webhooks** or scheduled CSV.

## Alerts

Notify sales owners in Slack or email when a hot lead replies or books a meeting.
