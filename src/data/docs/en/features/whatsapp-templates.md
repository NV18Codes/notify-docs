# WhatsApp templates

Templates are required for business-initiated messages outside the 24-hour session window.

## Categories

- **Marketing** — promotions; priced at **₹0.8631** per conversation (see [Pricing](/docs/pricing)).
- **Utility / Authentication** — alerts & OTPs; priced at **₹0.115** per conversation.

## Lifecycle in Notifyy

1. Draft copy and variables in Meta Business Manager.
2. Sync template status into Notifyy.
3. Attach media and quick-reply buttons where supported.
4. Use in campaigns or chatbot steps once **approved**.

## Best practices

- Keep body text under recommended length; test on multiple devices.
- Localize templates per region — Hindi, Kannada, and English variants are common for Indian audiences.
