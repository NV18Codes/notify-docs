# Contact management

Keep WhatsApp audiences clean with deduplication, enrichment, and consent flags.

## Profiles

Each contact stores:

- Phone (E.164), name, and locale.
- Tags and custom fields synced from your CRM.
- **Opt-in status** per message category.

## Imports & sync

- CSV upload with column mapping validation.
- Periodic **CRM sync** (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho — plan-dependent).

## Segmentation

Combine filters (e.g. *city = Mumbai* AND *last order < 30d*) for precise campaign cohorts.
