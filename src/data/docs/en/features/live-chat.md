# Multi-agent live chat

Route WhatsApp conversations to the right teammate with full customer context.

## Queues & assignment

- Create **queues** by topic, language, or region.
- Use **round-robin**, **skill-based**, or **VIP** routing.
- Agents see **CRM cards** with orders, tickets, and notes.

## Collaboration

- **Private notes** and @mentions keep coordination inside the thread.
- **Supervisors** can monitor SLAs and barge-in when needed.

## Quality

Track **first response time**, **resolution time**, and **CSAT** collected after a session ends.
