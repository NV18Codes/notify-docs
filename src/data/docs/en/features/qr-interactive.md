# QR code & interactive messaging

Turn offline touchpoints into measurable WhatsApp journeys.

## QR flows

Generate **deep-linked QR codes** that open a pre-filled message or subscribe users to a list with consent capture.

## Interactive messages

Use **list messages**, **reply buttons**, and **flows** (where available) to reduce typing friction and lift conversion.

## Analytics

Attribute scans to campaigns, retail locations, or packaging variants — see scans → sessions → purchases in **Analytics**.
