# What is Notifyy?

Notifyy is a **WhatsApp-first CRM and marketing automation platform** built for revenue, support, and operations teams who need compliant, measurable messaging at scale.

## Why WhatsApp

With **3.1+ billion** users and **98% typical open rates**, WhatsApp is where your customers already spend time. Campaigns on Notifyy routinely see **50–70% click rates** and **~12× higher CTR** than email or SMS for the same creative — because messages arrive in a trusted, conversational channel.

## What you can build

- **Customer journeys** that combine broadcasts, chatbots, and human handoff.
- **Multi-agent live chat** with routing, SLAs, and context from CRM fields.
- **Template-driven outreach** that respects WhatsApp policies and regional pricing.

## How Notifyy is different

Notifyy connects the **WhatsApp Business Platform** to practical go-to-market workflows: approvals, analytics, contact hygiene, and agent tooling — without forcing your team to juggle five different tools.
